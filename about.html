About Us

Welcome to Janata Boarding School

Established in the heart of Kamalamai, Sindhuli, Janata Boarding School has been a pillar of education for decades. We are committed to academic excellence, moral values, discipline, and holistic development. Our dedicated faculty, modern teaching methods, and supportive environment ensure that every student is guided towards a brighter future.

Our Core Principles

Our Mission

To provide quality education that fosters creativity, innovation, and a love for lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading institution that nurtures responsible citizens and global leaders.

Our Values

Discipline, Respect, Integrity, Compassion, and Academic Excellence are the foundation of our school culture.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings, Janata Boarding School has grown into one of the most trusted schools in Sindhuli. Over the years, we have achieved milestones in academics, sports, cultural activities, and community service. Our alumni continue to excel nationally and internationally, carrying forward the values instilled in them here.
